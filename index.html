Upsilon Pi Epsilon

Upsilon Pi Epsilon is the International Honor Society for the Computing and Information Disciplines. Founded in 2013, the chapter here at Wash U's primary purpose is to honor academic excellence in the computing and information disciplines. However, we also intend to work with other student groups to organize events to benefit the local computer science community.

We'd like to extend our thanks to Answers.com for their help in starting this chapter.

If you are interested in joining, check out the requirements.
